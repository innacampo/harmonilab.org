Cognitive Audit Scope

The Three Silent Threat Detectors

CLARA meticulously scans clinical dialogue for specific patterns of reasoning failures that are most common in medical errors.

DS

Diagnostic Shadowing

Detects when physical symptoms are incorrectly attributed to a patient's pre-existing psychiatric history, leading to missed physical diagnoses.

PC

Premature Closure

The tendency to cease the diagnostic process before all possibilities have been explored and alternatives ruled out.

AB

Anchoring Bias

Flags the over-reliance on the first piece of information received, ignoring subsequent evidence that contradicts the initial impression.
